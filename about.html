Welcome to this section

I am Kirti Kapoor, a student who obsesses over books and art. Currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Psychology,I like pushing my limits and to try new things. This page is all about the things I love and would like you to know about! While reading is definitely on the top of my list because language is a beautiful art form, I also love the symmetry that comes with drawing a mandala and the flow that paints create with each stroke on the blank pages of my book. So be ready for some imperfectly awesome paintings in the gallery section!
